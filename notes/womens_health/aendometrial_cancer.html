Patient handout - Endometrial cancer - patient.co.uk

Clinical features

  • Peak incidence 50-70yo

  • Risk factors:

    • age

    • obestiy

    • nulliparity

    • late menopause

    • diabetes mellitus

    • polycystic ovary

    • drugs

      • unopposed oestrogen

      • tamoxifen

    • family history

      • breast

      • ovarian

      • colon cancer

Symptoms

80% present with abnormal bleeding

Especially post menopausal bleeding

Investigations

  • Smear

    • detects some cases

    • not excluded by normal smear

  • Ultrasound

    • >12mm

  • Pipelle

Management

Urgent gynae referral
