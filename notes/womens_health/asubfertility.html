Sub-Fertility
  • Public funding
  • early referral

  • 20-25% chance of healthy couple of reproductive age becoming pregnant each reproductive cycle

  • 6-% within 6mo

  • 84% first year

  • 92% second year

  • address lifestyle

    • BMI\<18, >30

    • smoking

    • EtOH

  • Investigation

    • 12mo \<35yo

    • 6mo >35yo

    • both partners

  • Female with regular mestrual cycels

    • presume ovulation occuring

    • progesterone

      • 7d prior to expected date of menstruation to test if ovulation occurred

        • if >25 then likley

    • FSH, LH and oestradiol checked early in menstural cycles

      • d2-6

      • elevated FSH suggests reduced ovarian reserve

  • Male

    • semen

      • if abnormal repeat 6wk

Public funding

  • BMI 18-32

  • Non smoker or ex-smoker for >3mo

  • Age \<40

  • Less than 2 children from current relationships

  • at least 2 years subfertility

  • or

  • 1 year if age \<35 or 6mo if age >35 with

    • anovulation

    • azoospermia

    • oligospermia

    • bilateral salingectomy

    • tubal obstruction

    • oophrectomy

    • premature ovarian failure

early referral

  • female >35

  • female amenorrhoea/oligomenorrhoea

  • female partner history of abdominal/pelvic surgery

  • female partner abnormal pelvic exam

  • femal/male history of STI

  • male abnormal semen

  • male undescended testes or other genital pathology

  • varicocele

  • urogenital surgery

  • couple are very concerned and likely to benefit from reassurance
