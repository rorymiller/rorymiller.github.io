LNG-IUS
  • risks

  • progestogenic effect on endometrium

    • prevents implantation

  • within 1 month

    • high intrauterine concentrations induce endometrial atrophy

  • little effect on HPO axis

  • majority of women continue to ovulate

  • failure rate \<1%

  • licensed for 5 years contraceptive use

  • licensed for 4 years to provide endometrial protection

  • if inserted >45yo then can have up to menopause

  • can be inserted any time in menstrual cycle if not pregnant and no risk of conceptoin

    • condoms or abstinence advised for 7d after unless in first 7 days
