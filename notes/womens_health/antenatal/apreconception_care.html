Pre-conception check

  • state of woman’s health direct effect on health of foetus

  • 30-50% unplanned

Nutrition

Folic acid

  • ≤ 4 weeks before conception

  • first 12 weeks of pregnancy

  • reduces risk of neural tube defects

  • daily supp of 800mcg folic acid

  • higher doses 5mg/day for

    • previous neural tube defect,

    • fhx of neural tube defects,

    • anticonvulsant use

    • women with DM

    • obese

Iodine

  • changes due to thyroid

  • 150mcg of postassium iodate

Iron

  • increase substantially after first trimester

Vit D

  • lack of vit d adversely affect foetal bone and accumulation of newborn vit d stores

  • rickets

    • pre-prepared salad
