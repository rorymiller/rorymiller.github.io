Encopresis

Encopresis
  • features
  • Management

involuntary passage of formed or semi0formed stools into underwear

occuring repeatedly for ≥ 1mo over 4yo

cause physiological (= poor diet)

features

  • 1-2/100

  • boys > girls 3:1

  • inadequate toileting

  • poor diet

  • faecal retntion

  • rectal dilatation and insensitive urge to stool

  • unawareness of passage of stool

  • enuresis common

Management

  • structured toileting program

  • initial task = empty bowel of faeces

  • majority cured by:

    • ongoing interest and support

    • education

    • good noraml diet, adequate fluids, exercise

    • structured toileting program

      • reuglar sitting on toilet at least 10min 3times/day after each meal

    • laxative

    • senokot granules

    • if unsuccessful:

      • sodium phosphate enema if >2yo

    • starcheart with successful results

    • regular follow-up

    • consider encopresis clinic
