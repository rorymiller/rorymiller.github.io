Toilet training

Toilet training
  • General rules
  • Key points:

  • use toilet when they are ready

  • fully trained:

    • daytime; 2 1/2 - 4yo

    • nighttime; by 8yo

  • once start training - 4mo before they are dry

    • some can take several months

General rules

  • be relaxed

  • avoid rushing

  • do not force child to go to toilet

  • nagging doesn’t work well

  • punishing won’t work

  • best time to sit on toilet

    • first thing in morning

    • after meals

    • when sense their need to go

    • before going out

    • upon retunring

Key points:

  • use potty or toilet with seat ring and step

  • explain process

  • sit both boys nad girls to pass urine

  • make a fuss of success

  • help child relax

  • stop using nappies

  • if training upsetting - wait a month before trying again
