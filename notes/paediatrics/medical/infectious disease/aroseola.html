Roseola

Roseola
  • Clinical

  • sixth disease

  • HHV - 6 and HHV-7

Clinical

  • high fever (abrupt onset)

    • lasts 3-5d

  • maculopapular rash

    • rose coloured

    • lasts 1-3d

  • URTI symptoms may occur

  • GI symptoms described

  • most children are irritable and appear toxic

  • associated with approx 1/3 of febrile seizures
