Hand, foot and mouth/ Coxsackie

Hand, foot and mouth/ Coxsackie
  • microbiology
  • Syndrome
  • Exclusion
  • complications

microbiology

  • enterovirus: coxsackie virus A16

  • coughing or poor hand washing

  • direct spread from an infected person

  • incubation 3-5d

  • infection while unwell and longer

Syndrome

  • fever

  • rash:

    • images - dermnet

    • soles

    • palms

    • mouth

      • sometimes painful

    • flat pink patches -> small enlongated greyish blisters

  • flu like illness

Exclusion

exclusion from kindergarten/school

  • while child is feeling unwell

  • unnecessary if child is well

complications

  • enteritis

  • myocarditis

  • meninoencephalitis

  • acute flaccid paralyiss

  • pulmonary oedema and pneumonia

  • pregnancy

    • first trimester spontanuosu abortion or fetal growth retardation
