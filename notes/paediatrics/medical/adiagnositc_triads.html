Acute/subacute

  • Arthralgia + rash +/- abdo pain

    • Henoch-Schonlein purpura

  • Resp symptoms + vomiting + mental changes (seizure/coma)

    • Reye syndrome

  • Pallor + drowsineess + fever

    • Meningitis

  • Mailaise + fever + polyarthritis (rash, chorea)

    • Rheumatic fever

  • \<3mo - male - wekaness + weight loss + vomiting

    • Pyloric stenosis

  • neonate

    • vomiitng after first feeds, drooling, abdominal distension

      • oesophageal/duodenal atresia

  • malaise, pallor, bone pain

    • acute leukaemia (lymphocytic)

  • during gastro

    • malaise, diarrhoea, severe abdominal pain

      • Haemolytic uraemic syndrome

Developmental delay

    • fragile X syndrome

  • small extremeties + narrow forehead + eating disorder

    • prader-willi syndrome

  • elfin face, low set ears, cardiac murmur

    • williams syndrome

  • short, webbed neck, pigmented naevi, caridacd disprder

    • turner 

  • short , webbed neck, facial disproportion, caridac
