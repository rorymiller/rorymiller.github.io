Hernia

Hernia
  • Inguinal
  • Umbilical hernia
  • para-umbilical
  • Epigastric hernia

Inguinal

  • present 1st 3-4mo

  • incidence 1:50M, 1:500F

  • referred urgently

  • early surgery

    • 6-2 rule:

      • birth - 6wk: surgery 2d

      • 6wk - 6mo; surgery within 2wk

      • over 6,o; surgery within 2mo

Umbilical hernia

  • not usually require

  • most close naturally by 4yo

  • refer if still presnet @ 4yo

  • if hernial orifice >1cm at 12mo then intervention is posibillity

para-umbilical

  • defect in linea alba

    • adjacent to umbilicus proper

  • most lie just above umbilicus

  • elliptical slit with firm edges

  • spont. closure rarely occurs

    • more likley to incarecerate

  • refer 6-12mo

Epigastric hernia

  • umbilicus and xiphisternum

  • unlikely to close

  • likley incarcerate

  • causes pain by strangulation of herniated fat
