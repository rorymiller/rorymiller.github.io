most common form of arthritis

leading cause of pain and disability around the world

\~50% of people >60yo

Factors;

  • genetic factors

  • age

  • joint damage by injury

  • joint damaage by chronic obesity

  • knee, hip, spine, hand

Treatment

  • no treatemnt that can reverse joint damage

  • early management can slow disease progress

  • symptom control

  • glucosamine

    • 1500mg / day provides pain relief perhaps

    • recent cochrane

      • may not improve mobility

First tier:

  • Knowledge/education

  • exercise

  • weight loss (if overweight)

Second tier - safe pharmacological agents:

  • Paracetamol

  • Topical NSAIDs

  • Capsaicin cream

    • 0.025% - chilli pepper extract

    • use four times/day

Third tier

  • oral NSAIDs

    • evidence superior to paracetamol for pain relief

  • Opioids

  • intra-articular corticosteroid injection

    • moderate severe apin that doesn’t respond adequately to oral treatment

    • no more than 4x/year per joint
